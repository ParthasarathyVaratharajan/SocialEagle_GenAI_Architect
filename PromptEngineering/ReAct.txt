ReAct = Reasoning + Acting
Itâ€™s a prompt strategy where the model:
- Thinks step by step (like Chain of Thought)
- Takes actions (e.g., calling tools, querying APIs, retrieving documents)
- Reflects on results
- Repeats the cycle until a final answer is reached
This technique is ideal for:
- Multi-step decision making
- Tool-augmented workflows (e.g., search, calculator, database)
- Autonomous agents and multi-modal systems
- Debugging, planning, and retrieval tasks
