üß† How It Works
- Decompose the problem into intermediate steps (thoughts).
- Branch from each thought with multiple continuations.
- Evaluate each branch based on criteria like coherence, progress, or utility.
- Prune weak paths, continue exploring strong ones.
- Select the best path to arrive at the final answer.
This mimics human problem-solving: we brainstorm, explore options, discard dead ends, and refine our thinking
